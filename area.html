FFF BREWING

In the heart of the city, you’ll find many small brewers to discover. But a great way to find all of these delicious local brews is to hop on a craft brewery tour. Drive or walk around the city with a guide and get acquainted with the budding craft beer scene!

VALIO

The Valio Show Dairy, with its village character, is an attractive place to learn and discover. The little cheese village is a blend of the past and the present. You'll experience both traditional and modern production techniques from the world-famous Valio Cordoza.

RED & BLUE

Red & Blue is the place for trendy, fashionable parties and famous names, offering a refreshing and unique weekly programme with national and international DJs. Collaboration with international concepts such as Pacha, MTV and Hed Kandi provides an even greater club sensation. Minimum age: 21+

THINGS TO DO

- View our list of things to do in the Keys during your vacation at our private cabins.


Back Country Fishing

The backcountry of the South Keys is the perfect stage for any sport-fishing enthusiast or beginner. While silently polling across the beautiful, expansive waters, you look for the elusive “BIG 3” – tarpon, permit and bonefish. Imagine the thrill of hooking one of these legendary fighters. Trips normally go out from 9 am – 1 pm or 1 pm – 5 pm for half days, but we are more than happy to accommodate any special requests for specific times.


*Don’t forget polarized glasses, sunscreen and a hat for full enjoyment and protection.

Scuba Diving Trip

Our trips to Looe Key Reef afford you the opportunity to float weightlessly among all the colorful creatures that make this beautiful reef their home. Because of its relatively shallow depth, the reef is a great dive site for divers new to the sport, experienced divers, and for those who have been out of the water for some time. We have professional Scuba instructors that guide every dive to maximize your experience.

Sailing

Come aboard the Island’s 38’ Admiral Executive Yacht built in South Africa. Join us on this Catamaran, with full bar, and let our captain teach you the true art of relaxation. Your choices of sails are available, and can be reserved for either private parties or groups up to six. Choose a complimentary bottle of red or white wine, accompanied by a fruit & cheese platter. Our Day Sails can be scheduled according to your time preference since it will be a private cruise. This trip can include snorkeling gear and fish trolling during the excursion. We can also arrange Full Moon and “Under the Stars” sails.



Seaplane

Arrive direct to Little Palm Island via private seaplane charter with Tropic Ocean Airways. Fly direct to Little Palm Island from airports in mainland. Connecting from major carriers to your private seaplane charter is easy and stress-free. Enjoy the magnificent views over the shoreline en route to the Keys.


For Seaplane Reservations: For reservations please call Tropic Ocean Airways at xxx-210-xxxx or send an e-mail to reservations@fly.com

Jungle Tours

Indulge yourself in a natural history and sightseeing excursion into the Great White Heron Wildlife Refuge, the 2nd largest migration path of birds in the U.S. Take the binoculars from your room and prepare for a breathtaking day of observing nature at its finest!

Sky Dive

Make a tandem skydive from two miles high over our incredible South Keys. While climbing to altitude you will see the spectacular views of Key West, the Atlantic Ocean. After jumping from the plane, you will experience the thrill of falling at speeds of 130 mph or more. Once your parachute opens, you will enjoy five to seven minute ride floating quietly back to earth for a soft, pinpoint landing.


*You must pay Sky Dive Key West directly, we cannot charge your folio for this activity.
